# Dataset 6 — Population by Age Group, Sex, Region, and Province/HUC (Philippines, 2020)

## Source
Philippine Statistics Authority (2020 Census of Population and Housing)

## Description
This dataset provides the total population disaggregated by:
- Region
- Province / Highly Urbanized City (HUC)
- Sex (Both Sexes, Male, Female)
- Age group (detailed, 0–80+)

It enables demographic analysis, dependency ratios, gender distribution, and regional age structure.
